Activity ID: A20
Activity Name: Participate in a discussion with your friends about cybersecurity event.

Objective: To articulate and discuss real-world cybersecurity incidents with peers, demonstrating an understanding of threat vectors and business impacts.

Methodology (How I did it): I initiated a conversation with two friends in a Discord group chat regarding the massive MGM Resorts cyberattack. I guided the discussion to focus on the specific, non-technical attack vector used by the threat actors.

Results & Findings: I explained that the breach, which cost the company approximately $100 million, was not caused by a highly technical brute-force attack on the network. Instead, it was executed through "vishing" (voice phishing) and social engineering. We discussed how the attackers used open-source intelligence (LinkedIn) to find an employee's name and manipulated the IT helpdesk into resetting their credentials. This highlighted to my friends that the human element is often the most critical vulnerability in an enterprise security posture, rendering even the most expensive firewalls useless if the "keys" are simply handed over.
