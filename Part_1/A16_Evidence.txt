Activity ID: A16
Activity Name: Discover 3 local security incidents.

Objective: To research and document three recent, significant cybersecurity incidents that directly impacted local Western Australian organizations and infrastructure.

Methodology (How I did it): I conducted Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) gathering using search engines and local cyber-news publications to identify data breaches and ransomware attacks specific to Western Australia within the last two years. I captured screenshots of the news reports as evidence.

Results & Findings: I identified three major local incidents:

UWA Password Breach (Aug 2025): Unauthorized access to UWA's password systems forced a campus-wide IT lockout and mandatory password resets for all students and staff.

LPBWA Ransomware Attack (May 2025): The Dire Wolf ransomware gang breached the Legal Practice Board of WA, stealing 300GB of sensitive practitioner data and crippling their online services.

Harry Perkins Institute Breach (July 2024): The Perth-based medical research facility suffered a severe breach resulting in the alleged leak of 4TB of internal data.
